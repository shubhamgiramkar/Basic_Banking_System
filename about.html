About Us

The TSF is a leading independent child organisation, which works in many states of the country. It's established in 2000.


EDUCATION IS MUST TO ALL !!


An Initiative to provide education during covid pandemic.


Our foundation aim to providing support as much as possible to the poor childern.


Our main objective is to provide free education, books, tuition and nutrition to the
childern whose parents inspite of working hard are not able to afford facilites for their children .


We try to ensure that every child gets equal chances and opportunities.
